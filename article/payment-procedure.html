Payment procedure tutorial

This tutorial will guide you to make a payment. If you have any difficulties with this method, you can also email the receipt to us.

1. Go to the ICST UGM PRESENTER site and click FEES.

2. Find out how much do you have to pay by clicking CALCULATOR. Please use this online calculator to know the total amount you need to pay.

How to use online calculator for presenter:

  1. Select type of participation as presenter
  2. Select your country
  3. Select your status as student or non-student
  4. Select IEEE Membership as IEEE member or non-member
  5. Enter how many papers you are going to present
  6. Click Count

How to use online calculator for non-presenter:

  1. Select type of participation as non-presenter
  2. Select your country
  3. Click Count

The calculation result shows the amount should be paid.

3. Make a payment*.

Payments cannot be made in cash; they must be via credit card or bank transfer to the ICST UGM account below:

For all local participants, please transfer to:

Account number  9888800224111453

Account name  UGM ICST

Bank name  BNI 46

For foreign participants (Material Science and Chemistry symposium only), please transfer to:

Account number  9888900224111453

Account name  UGM ICST

Bank name  BNI 46

Swift code  BNINIDJA

For foreign participants, the payment is made via credit card in EDAS system.

* If you wish to pay using methods other than those mentioned above, please consult us.

4. Confirm your payment.

Please make sure to confirm your payment by filling the confirmation form for presenter otherwise you cannot submit your camera-ready manuscript and confirm your payment by filling the confirmation form for non-presenter .

Please prepare your payment receipt and enter your details.

Additional file for Presenter (optional):
  • International participant: student ID
  • Local participant: IEEE membercard
